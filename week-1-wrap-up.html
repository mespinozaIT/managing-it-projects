üí≠ Things to Think About...

As we wrap up Week 1, here are some important concepts to start thinking about for next week:

üéØ Elevator Pitches

Start thinking about how you'd describe yourself professionally in 30 seconds.

An elevator pitch is a brief, persuasive speech that sparks interest in what you do. As future project managers, you'll need to:

  • Clearly communicate your value proposition
  • Connect with stakeholders quickly and effectively
  • Make a memorable first impression

What makes you unique? What value do you bring to a project team?

üíº LinkedIn First Impressions

Your photo and profile headline are crucial - they're the first things people notice.

In the professional world, your LinkedIn presence often serves as your first introduction. Consider:

  • Does your profile photo look professional and approachable?
  • Is your headline clear about what you do and aspire to do?
  • Would someone immediately understand your professional value?

Take a moment to review your current LinkedIn profile - what impression does it make?
