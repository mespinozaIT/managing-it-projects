Project Management Principles

Stewardship
Be responsible and ethical in managing the project.
Team
Build a respectful and accountable team.
Stakeholders
Keep interested parties informed and involved.
Value
Focus on delivering important benefits.
Systems Thinking
Understand how project parts interact.
Leadership
Guide and inspire the team positively.
Tailoring
Adapt methods to fit the project needs.
Quality
Ensure work meets high standards.
Complexity
Manage and simplify complicated aspects.
Risk
Identify and prepare for potential problems.
Adaptability and Resilience
Stay flexible and recover from setbacks.
Change Management
Help people adjust to project changes.

Project Performance Domains

Stakeholders
Understanding who is interested in the project and making sure they are happy with how things are going. It involves talking to them and getting their input to make the project successful.
Team
Focuses on the people working on the project. It's about building a strong team that works well together, respects each other, and takes responsibility for their tasks.
Development Approach and Life Cycle
Choosing the right way to do the project. It means picking a method that fits the project's goals and the way the team works best.
Planning
Figuring out what needs to be done, how to do it, and who will do it. It's like making a roadmap for the project to ensure everything is organized and on track.
Project Work
The actual doing part of the project. It involves carrying out the tasks and activities needed to create the project's deliverables.
Delivery
Ensures that the project's results are handed over to the people who need them, and that they meet the expected quality standards.
Measurement
Keeping an eye on how the project is going. It involves checking if everything is on track and making changes if needed to stay on course.
Uncertainty
Identifying things that could go wrong and planning how to deal with them. It's about being prepared for surprises and finding ways to turn challenges into opportunities.

Remember

The PMBOK Guide provides a comprehensive framework for project management success. These principles and domains work together to create a holistic approach to managing projects effectively across all industries.
