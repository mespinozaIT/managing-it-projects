Mission, Vision & Strategy

Understanding the Foundation of Organizational Direction

Why These Three Matter in IT Projects

Every successful IT project must align with organizational direction. Mission, Vision, and Strategy provide the framework that guides project decisions, priorities, and success metrics. Understanding how they work together ensures your projects deliver real business value.

For IT Project Managers

Mission Alignment

Ensure your project serves the organization's core purpose. Ask: "Does this project help us fulfill our reason for existing?"

Vision Support

Projects should move the organization closer to its desired future state. Ask: "How does this get us where we want to be?"

Strategic Implementation

Your project should execute part of the strategic plan. Ask: "How does this project advance our competitive strategy?"

Notice the Pattern

Each company's Mission defines their core purpose, their Vision paints where they want to be, and their Strategy outlines how they'll compete and win in their market. Every IT project should connect to this hierarchy!

Key Takeaway for IT Project Managers

Before starting any project, ask: "How does this align with our Mission, support our Vision, and execute our Strategy?" Projects that clearly connect to all three are more likely to get approval, resources, and stakeholder support.
