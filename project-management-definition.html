Project Management is NOT...

Just making a Gantt chart
Tools help, but they don't manage the project‚Äîyou do.
Telling people what to do
PM is about influence, not control. You guide, support, and empower.

Projects exist in every field: marketing, healthcare, education, nonprofits, etc.
Just about "checking boxes"

Always linear
